## The Appeal of At-Home Wax Strips

Drugstore wax strips are tempting for good reasons: convenience, privacy, and a lower upfront cost of $8-$15 per box versus $25-$60 per professional session. But that convenience comes with serious trade-offs.

## Where At-Home Wax Strips Fall Short
